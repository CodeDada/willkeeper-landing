WillKeeper is integrating with India's Account Aggregator (AA) ecosystem as a licensed TSP (Technology Service Provider). Your family won't miss a single account — every bank, mutual fund, insurance policy, and provident fund is automatically discovered with your consent.

🏭

All Institutions, Automatically

Banks, NBFCs, mutual funds, insurance, EPFO — WillKeeper pulls a complete picture via AA-connected institutions (FIPs), with zero manual entry.

🔒

Consent-First, Always

Nothing moves without your explicit approval. You control what is shared, with whom, and for how long — and you can revoke access at any time.

RBI-Regulated Infrastructure

The AA framework is governed by RBI and covers banks (RBI), insurers (IRDAI), mutual funds (SEBI), and pensions (PFRDA).
